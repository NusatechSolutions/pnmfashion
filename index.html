Frequently Asked

Got Questions? We've Got You Covered.

What types of fabrics do you supply? +
We stock a wide variety of woven and knitted textiles including cotton (plain and printed), polyester, polyester-cotton blends, viscose, rayon, linen, satin and our signature traditional ranges — songket, Indian sarees, kaftans and abaya.
How do I place a wholesale order? +
The quickest way is to WhatsApp us with the items or fabric categories you're interested in. We'll respond with current stock, specifications, MOQs and pricing within the same business day. Visits to our Campbell Complex showroom are also welcome by appointment.
Do you offer fabric samples? +
Yes. We can prepare swatches for serious enquiries, particularly when an order is being considered. WhatsApp us with your requirements and we'll arrange sample dispatch.
What are your delivery options? +
Local Klang Valley deliveries are handled by our own van fleet. For Peninsular Malaysia, we work with trusted courier partners. East Malaysia and overseas shipments are coordinated through approved logistics handlers.
Can you do custom printing or dyeing? +
Yes. Custom print runs and dye matching are available for brands and designers. MOQ terms apply — get in touch via WhatsApp with your design and quantity, and we'll prepare a quotation.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept Visa and Mastercard (credit and debit), instant bank transfers and cash payments at our showroom. For larger orders, deposit arrangements can be discussed.
What is your return policy? +
Returns are accepted within 5 days of purchase with original receipt, provided the fabric has not been cut from any edge and remains in its original packaging.
